# Chapter 1 scenes

## Scene 1 — The dark beacon

Mara climbs the wet tower and finds the lamp empty.

## Scene 2 — The messenger

Tomas arrives breathless: a boat is trapped below the bend. The magistrate is
aboard. Mara hears the name and closes the lantern door.

## Scene 3 — The ledger

Mara finds her brother's name in the condemnation ledger, written in the
magistrate's hand.

## Scene 4 — The light

Mara lights the beacon. The boat turns toward the safe channel.

## Scene 5 — No thanks

At dawn Mara refuses the magistrate's payment, keeps the ledger, and repairs
the tower for the next flood.
